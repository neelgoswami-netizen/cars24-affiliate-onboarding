ABOUT Cars24

Cars24 is one of the leading digital platforms for buying and selling pre-owned vehicles. The platform facilitates transactions for over 1 million cars every year. Cars24 has simplified the entire car selling and buying experience for customers and partners.

Founded in 2015, Cars24 operates across multiple markets including India, UAE, and Australia, enabling customers to sell their cars from home and buyers to purchase quality-checked vehicles through a trusted marketplace.

Cars24 leverages data science, AI-driven pricing, and a strong logistics network to make car transactions fast, transparent, and hassle-free.

Partnership Overview

Role of Affiliate Partners

Affiliate Partners are an important part of the Cars24 growth ecosystem. They help to connect car sellers with the Cars24 platform by generating leads for buying and selling the car and supporting the overall vehicle acquisition process.

Through this partnership, affiliates not only contribute to increasing the supply of cars on the platform but also benefit from a scalable earning opportunity, transparent performance tracking, and the chance to grow alongside one of the leading digital automotive marketplaces.

The Affiliate Partner Program provides partners with the opportunity to earn attractive performance-based incentives, depending on their operational efficiency and overall contribution to the marketplace.

KPI Rules

Payout Conditions

For KPI-linked payouts (Seller & Buyer), your performance directly determines your rate.

✅

All KPIs Achieved → Maximum Fixed CPI / CPV

When all KPIs meet or exceed baselines, you earn the full fixed rate — ₹1,400 (Seller) or ₹1,000 (Buyer). No reductions applied.

⚠️

One or More KPIs Missed → Pro-Rata Rate

Missing any single KPI reduces your payout proportionally. Other achieved KPIs still contribute fully — only the missed metric is reduced.

❌

All KPIs Missed → Significant Rate Reduction

If all KPIs fall below baseline simultaneously, the combined multiplier effect causes a significant drop — well below the maximum rate.
